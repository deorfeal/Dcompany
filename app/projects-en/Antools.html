1. About the client

Antools - specialized landing that representing tools that Significantly facilitate the process of creating design and development.The platform provides a variety of solutions for professionals in the field of design and development, improving quality of work and increasing the efficiency of the process.Antools is focused on users seeking to accelerate and simplify their activities with the help of convenient and functional tools.

2. Goals and tasks

A modern landing was developed for Antools, which is clearly and affordable demonstrates the advantages of the proposed tools.In the process of work Particular attention was paid to layout: the creation of structured and lightweight interface using HTML5, SCSS, BEM, GULP, and also Integration of dynamic elements using JavaScript and JQuery to increase interactivity and improvement of user experience.

I offer the creation of high-quality websites, including landing pages, corporate websites, online stores, and web applications. My goal is to create user-friendly, responsive, and efficient solutions that provide the best experience for users and help businesses grow. Contact me for further communication.

3. Steps of execution

  • Development of page structure (creating a convenient and logical architecture for instrument representations)
  • Interface design (development of modern and attractive visual style)
  • Layout and adaptation (use HTML5, SCSS, BEM and GULP to ensure adaptability and structuredness)
  • Interactive elements (implementation of dynamic functions using JavaScript and jquery)
  • Performance optimization (acceleration of page loading and improving interface response)
  • Testing and launching (checking the correctness of the work of all functions and successful Site launch)
